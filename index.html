3 SDG

About SDG 3


SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. This goal addresses major health priorities including reproductive, maternal, and child health; communicable, non-communicable, and environmental diseases; universal health coverage; and access to safe, effective, quality, and affordable medicines and vaccines.

Good health is essential to sustainable development and the 2030 Agenda reflects the complexity and interconnectedness of the two.

Key Targets

SDG 3 encompasses 13 targets focusing on different aspects of health and well-being

Reduce Maternal Mortality

End preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5 years of age

End Epidemics

Combat AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases

Promote Mental Health

Strengthen prevention and treatment of substance abuse and mental health

Universal Health Coverage

Achieve universal health coverage including financial risk protection
